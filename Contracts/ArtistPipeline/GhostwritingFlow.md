# ✍️ Flame Division — Ghostwriting & Lyric Creation Flow  
*Draft operational template.*

## 1. Intake Form
Artist provides:
- Theme  
- Target emotion  
- Flow style  
- Genre  
- Sample references  
- Length (hook, verse, full song)  

---

## 2. Direction & Ethics
All ghostwriting:
- Respects the artist’s identity  
- Maintains transparency between internal teams  
- Protects the writer’s authorship unless contracted otherwise  

**Flame Law:**  
> “Words are identity — handle them with respect.”

---

## 3. Draft Creation
Options:
- Human-written  
- Hybrid human + AI  
- Full AI draft guided by prompts  

Delivered as:
- Hook  
- Verse  
- Full song  
- Ad-lib ideas  

---

## 4. Revisions
Artist may request:
- Tone changes (aggressive, uplifting, darker, cleaner)  
- Structure changes  
- Flow adjustments  
- Additional metaphors or punchlines  

---

## 5. Ownership Selection
Artist chooses one:

### A. **Work-for-Hire**
- Artist owns 100%  
- Writer waived credit  

### B. **Shared Credit**
- Writer credited  
- Split defined in Royalty Ledger  

### C. **Anonymous Ghostwriting**
- Writer hidden  
- Artist assumes full public authorship  
- Private ledger retains true authorship  

---

## 6. Final Delivery
- PDF + TXT lyrics  
- Performance notes  
- Flow guide (optional audio mockup)  
- License or authorship contract  

---

## 7. Compliance
All works logged in:
- /Contracts  
- /ProjectLedger  
- /BrandSecurity (if needed for authorship protection)
