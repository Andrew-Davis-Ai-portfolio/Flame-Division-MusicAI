## 1. Purpose
This policy defines what constitutes unauthorized use of Flame Division assets and outlines the response procedures.

## 2. Prohibited Actions
The following actions are strictly prohibited without express written permission:

- Copying, selling, or distributing Flame Division assets  
- Using branding, logos, or emblems in third-party products  
- Training models on Flame Division materials  
- Uploading material to platforms under false identity  
- Claiming ownership of AI-assisted works created under direction  
- Editing or repackaging assets for resale or public release  
- Unauthorized YouTube/Spotify uploads
