## 2. Pre-Release Phase (7–21 Days)
This phase builds anticipation and sets the foundation for release momentum.

### Step 1 — Finalize Assets
- Final mix & master  
- Cover art + alt covers  
- Promo images  
- Visual snippet / teaser  
- EPK updated (optional)  

### Step 2 — Upload & Metadata
- Distributor upload  
- ISRC/UPC codes checked  
- Credits added  
- Lyrics uploaded  
- Smartlink generated  

### Step 3 — Teasers & Hints
- 1–2 short snippet clips  
- Caption: “Something’s coming…”  
- Light hashtag pack  
- Soft engagement in comments

### Step 4 — Playlist Preparation
- Identify curator list  
- Draft pitches  
- Submit early where possible  

---

## 3. Release Day (Day 0)
The goal is impact, clarity, and clean presentation. No spam.

### Step 1 — Official Announcement
- Post cover + smartlink
- Use Release Day Hashtag Pack
- Caption: direct, simple, intentional
- Pin post (if platform allows)

### Step 2 — Platform Push
- IG Reels + TikTok snippet
- YouTube visualizer or static upload
- Update all bios with smartlink
- Send EPK to key contacts

### Step 3 — Engagement Phase
- Reply to early comments
- Repost supporter stories
- Keep energy calm but present
- No overposting; quality > noise

---

## 4. Post-Release Week (Days 1–7)
Goal: maintain momentum without oversaturation.

### Step 1 — Content Cycle
- 1 performance clip
- 1 lyric or caption-style clip
- 1 behind-the-scenes moment
- 1 identity-driven photo post

### Step 2 — Micro-Stories
Share:
- Meaning behind the track
- Creation moment
- Personal message

### Step 3 — Playlist & Outreach
- DM or email curators (professional tone)
- Submit to blogs/playlists
- Light networking outreach

### Step 4 — Analytics Check (Day 7)
- Streams  
- Saves  
- Shares  
- Watch time (YT/TikTok)  
- Engagement rate  

Record results in the **Project Ledger**.

## 5. Long-Term Momentum (Days 8–30)
Keep the record alive without burning out your audience or yourself.

### Step 1 — Second Wave Content
- Acoustic or alternative version clip
- Visualizer or motion graphic
- Collab promo (if applicable)
- Fan reaction reposts

### Step 2 — Strategic Posts
- “What this track means to me” post
- “How the beat was made” clip
- “Studio build-up” memories
- “New listeners welcome” post

### Step 3 — Network Expansion
- Reach out to 3–5 new artists weekly
- Offer remixes, duets, or collabs
- Maintain respectful, professional tone

### Step 4 — Performance Prep
If performing soon:
- Lock setlist
- Update EPK
- Share a “rehearsal” story snippet

---

## 6. Monthly Review (Day 30)
Use this section to evaluate the track’s impact.

### Metrics to Record
- Total streams
- Saves & playlists added
- Social follower growth
- Engagement rates
- Best performing platform
- Audience location insights

### Adjustment Notes
Document what worked, what didn’t, and what to refine next release.
