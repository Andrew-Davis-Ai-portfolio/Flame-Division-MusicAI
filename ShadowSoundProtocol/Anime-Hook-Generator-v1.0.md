High-Emotion Vocal Hook Generator · Flame Division MusicAI  
Engineered for anime openings, AMV energy, cinematic vocals, and emotional power hooks.

## 1. System Purpose
The Anime-Hook-Generator is built to create:
- High-impact chorus hooks  
- Emotional pre-hooks  
- Anime-style power lines  
- J-pop / K-pop melodic phrases  
- AMV-ready theme vocals  

Its core function: mix lyrical intensity with cinematic melody patterns.

### **Feature A — Emotion Engine**

- Heroic

### **Feature B — Melody Shape Generator**
Forms unique anime hook contours:

### **Feature C — Theme Sync**
Aligns the hook with:

### **Feature E — Vocal Cadence Map**
Suggests vocal rhythm patterns matching:
- Pop

- K-pop dance lines  
- Ballad power choruses

## 3. Command Syntax

### **Standard Anime Hook**

/hook anime emotion:heroic bpm:160 key:Aminor

### **Tragic Rising Theme**

/hook anime emotion:tragic direction:ascending bpm:150

### **K-Pop Hyper Bounce**

/hook kpop bounce:high bpm:130 mode:hybrid

### **AMV Power Line Only**

/hook powerline mood:destiny length:short

### **Language Blend**

/hook anime language:jp_en emotion:victory
